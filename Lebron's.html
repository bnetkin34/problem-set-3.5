Advantages of Lebron's

  • They are usually high tops, preventing the ankle from rolling.
  • The Lebron's are often made of a sturdy flyknit material that allow for the shoe to last a long time.
  • They're are great basketball shoes, as they supply strong traction and grip.

Disavantages of Lebron's

  • They are extremely expensive. It is unlikely that a consumer will find a pair of Lebron's for under one hundred fifty dollars.
  • They are quite thick, possibly causing one's feet to heat up while playing.
  • Wearing them outside of the basketball could cause the traction and grip of the shoe to weaken.
