Advantages of Jordan's

  • They're stylish and can be worn with almost any outfit.
  • Jordan's can be worn for basketball.
  • Jordan's are a range of prices, depending on the amount of money the customer is willing to spend.

Disavantages of Jordan's

  • Jordan's do not take wear and tear well. They get dirty quite easily.
  • Jordan's crease easily, causing many customers to be unsatisfied with the product.
  • Many overspend on the Air Jordan brand due to its expensive nature.
